
Defining data columns for products:
-----------------------------------
'A': not used.
'B': name.
'C': default code.
'D': is saled (1) or nis not saled (0).
'E': is purchased (1) or nis not purchased (0).
'F': type.
'G': is actived (1) or not actived (0).
'H': routes.
'I': EAN 13 code.
'J': description.
'K': seller name.
'L': state.
'M': volume.
'N': weight.
'0': weight net.
'P': warranty.
'Q': sale delay.
'R': description sale.
'S': not used.
'T': website published (1) or not published (0) (specific for website_sale module).
'U': category name.
'V': length.
'W': high.
'X': width.
'Y': dimensional uom.
'Z': list price.
'AA': cost method.
'AB': standrad price.
'AC': purchase unit of measure.
'AD': unit of measure.
'AE': unit of sale.
'AF': unit coefficient.
'AG': seller delay.
'AH': seller minimal quantity.
'AI': seller pricelist minimal quantity.
'AJ': seller pricelist price.
'AK': orderpoint product minimum quantity.
'AL': orderpoint product maximum quantity.
'AM': orderpoint quantity multiple.
'AN': url ecommerce (specific for customized module).
'AO': url support (specific for customized module).
